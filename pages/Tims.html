About Taxation and Management Information Systems (TIMS)

ABOUT TIMS:

Taxation and Management Information Systems (TIMS) is a specialty that prepares future managers on how to manage a business and make informed decisions. This specialty spans through two broad sections.

The first section is dedicated to taxation where the various tax systems and taxes are explored within the context of the Cameroonian tax system. It also does an in-depth coverage of the liable persons, the declaration, and the payment of various taxes.

In the second section, the specialty introduces learners to management information systems. Learners will build competencies in the building and maintenance of management information systems.

WHO CAN STUDY TIMS?

TIMS is operated only at the second cycle and prepares students for the Advanced Technical and Vocational Education Examination organized by the CGCEB.

Prospective students need a background in Accounting, Computer studies, ICT, Mathematics, and other related subjects.

However, students must have a pass mark at the GCE Ordinary level/Intermediate Technical and Vocational Education Examination.

AFTER ADVANCED LEVEL, WHAT NEXT?

Students may further their studies in any of the following fields at a tertiary educational institution of their choice:
- Accounting- Taxation
- Computer Science- Software Engineering
- Cyber Security- Law (Tax Disputes)

JOB PROSPECTS AND POSITIONS FOR TIMS GRADUATES:

Graduates can occupy the following positions at both government or private organizations:
- Tax Accountant- Tax Auditor
- Payroll Manager- Database Administrator
- Tax Consultant- Web Master/Developer
- Financial Accountant- Information System Analyst
